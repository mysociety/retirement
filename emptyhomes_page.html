Looking for {{ page.siteName }}?

ReportEmptyHomes was a website created by mySociety for the Empty Homes Agency where users could report vacant or derelict houses.

The result was ReportEmptyHomes.com, a site which used the core FixMyStreet codebase, showing how this flexible software can be turned to many issue-mapping uses. Just as with FixMyStreet, the user could place a pin on a map, generating a report that the site would then send to the relevant council.

In 2011, the site was re-purposed for use alongside The Great British Property Scandal, a TV program developed by production agency Tiger Aspect for Channel 4 along with the Empty Homes Agency.

The project was a success and the programme was nominated for and Emmy and a BAFTA.

Why have we closed this site?

The TV series ran for two seasons and the commercial project came to an end. There are more details on the project in this case study on the mySociety website.

Reporting Empty Homes

If you would like to report an empty home in England, please contact your local council and ask for the contact details for the person who works on empty homes. You can also use the following link to report a derelict or abandoned building to the building control section of the local council: www.gov.uk/report-derelict-abandoned-building.

If you would like to report an empty home in Scotland and need to find out who to contact please visit the Shelter Scotland empty homes pages for a list of local Empty Homes Officers. If your council area doesn't appear on the Shelter Scotland site or you are having trouble directing your report to the right council contact please email the Scottish Empty Homes Partnership at emptyhomes@shelter.org.uk.

If you would like to report an empty homes in Wales, you can continue to report empty homes via the Shelter Cymru website: www.sheltercymru.org.uk/projects/empty-homes/.
