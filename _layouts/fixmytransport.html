How does it work?
 

Welcome to FixMyTransport

You can use this site to contact any transport operator in the UK - we'll send your message direct to their Customer Services department. We put it online, too. That way, others can read your problems, and offer support and advice.

And it works! Take a look at some of our recently fixed problems and find out why our users are saying:

  • Amazing - they've done it!
  • The conclusion of matters had a lot to do with my posting on your website after almost 2 months of inaction.
  • My bus service has really improved and I'm not the only one benefiting from this.
  • Thanks for your help in resolving this.
  • Well done and thanks for your assistance.

FixMyTransport is completely independent - we don't have any affiliation with any transport operators. See below for an explanation of how it all works.

And if you've got a problem with your transport? Go ahead and let the operators know.

How does it work?

We give you the tools to report your public transport problems to the correct operator or authority.

All reports and responses are posted online so that other people can support, respond and give advice.
