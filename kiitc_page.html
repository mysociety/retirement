Keep It In The Community was a mySociety project, underpinned by the FixMyStreet codebase, that collected data places of value to your local community, including Assets of Community Value and assets owned and run by the community.

Keep It In The Community (also known as KIITC, pronounced ‘Kitsy’) was a project to map Assets of Community Value (ACVs) and community-owned assets across England, and to allow anyone to highlight valued spaces and places that may become ACVs in the future.

KIITC was created by mySociety in collaboration with Power To Change, and it had two aims: to create an online record for each ACV and community-owned asset; and to provide an easy way to understand and compare the density, status, and demand for ACVs or community-owned assets in different regions of the country.

The Plunkett Foundation now manages the data on Community Assets formerly held on Keep It In The Community; helping support their wider campaign for community ownership of assets across the UK. Find out more at www.plunkett.co.uk.
